About Us

Fleek Image is an image consultant agency. We’re part of GaiGai (走街), an offline bespoke matchmaking agency who is wholly owned by Paktor. We’re committed to help individuals to create the best first impression. While you cannot control your physical features, you have total control over your dressing and grooming. We provide personalised one to one sessions with our experts to help you ensure your appearance convey who you are and how you want to be seen. We also regularly organize lively and dynamic workshops. Learn what works for you and your body type. When you look good, you feel good !

The Fleek Team

Phoebe found her passion in modeling and grooming and was crowned Miss Earth Singapore 2012 to represent Singapore amongst almost 90 other countries internationally. She brought her experience in international pageantry to train the various batches of Miss Earth Singapore beauty queens. Phoebe has also been a judge for Mrs Singapore World and Male Model Search (Singapore). She has a firm believer that there is beauty in everything and seeks to inspire and groom others to bring out their confidence in style while enhancing their natural personality.

– Phoebe Tan ( Image Consultant )

Diamond's passion lies in assisting men and women to achieve success through their image and wardrobe. She believes that everyone has the potential of possessing their own unique sense of personal style and dedicates herself to assisting clients in developing their full potential. With 8 years of experience in advertising, Diamond has a firm footing within the fashion and media industry and her globe-trotting sense of style contributes to her successes - making her highly sought after by her clientele without breaking the bank.

– Diamond Tan ( Fleek's Management )
